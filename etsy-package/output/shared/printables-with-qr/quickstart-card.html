Micro:bit Remote Builder

Browser BLE Control Panel for the BBC micro:bit V1 or V2
No install • No account • 100% local • EN / FR / AR
1
🤖

Flash the Firmware

Open makecode.microbit.org, switch to JavaScript, paste makecode.ts, hit Download and drag to your micro:bit V2.

2
🌐

Open the App

Double-click index.html in Chrome or Edge. No server, no install. Works offline as a PWA.

3
📡

Pair over BLE

Click Connect, pick BBC micro:bit [xxxxx] in the browser prompt. The board LEDs flash a tick.

4
🎛️

Play with the Tabs

LEDs, Sensors, Servos, GamePad, Graph, play tab, Bench, About. Press 1–8 to jump.

5
📈

Wrap Up

Open the Graph tab to see live temperature, light, sound and motion rolling across drag-and-drop. Press F for fullscreen, tap Record to capture a session and export it as JSON. No micro:bit? Try Simulate Mode.
