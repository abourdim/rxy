Long paragraph

rxy is a drag-drop Bluetooth remote builder for the BBC micro:bit — both V1 and V2. Classic micro:bit remote workflows require MakeCode, a custom BLE service, and wiring skill. rxy flattens that journey: pick from six templates (GamePad, Dashboard, Minimalist, Robotic, Scientific, Kid) or drag twelve widgets onto a canvas — Button, Slider, Joystick, D-Pad, XY Pad, Gauge, Graph, Battery, Timer, Knob, Switch, Readout. Click Connect to pair over Web Bluetooth; the board responds live as you adjust controls. One click exports paste-ready MakeCode JavaScript; one more downloads a ready-to-flash .hex. Five themes (Stealth, Neon, Arctic, Blaze, Classic). Trilingual UI (EN/FR/AR with full RTL). Twenty keyboard shortcuts for power users. Fully client-side — no cloud, no account, no telemetry, COPPA/GDPR-friendly from day one. Supporting both V1 and V2 is rare on the micro:bit remote market — most tools force V2-only due to BLE profile differences. One of the few no-code remote builders on Etsy that doesn't abandon V1 owners.
