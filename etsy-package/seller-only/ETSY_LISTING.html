FAQ

Do I need to know how to code?

No. Drag widgets onto a canvas, click 📄 Code, copy, paste into makecode.microbit.org, download, flash. The app writes every line of MakeCode for you.

Which micro:bit do I need?

BBC micro:bit V1 or V2, either works. USB cable or battery pack.

What browsers work?

Chrome or Edge on desktop, or Chrome on Android. Safari, Firefox, iPhone and iPad do NOT work — that's a Web Bluetooth limitation at the browser level, not a limitation of our app.

How does the micro:bit talk to the app?

BLE UART text messages. The app sends SET widget_id value; the micro:bit replies with UPD widget_id value to push numbers and text back to the remote.

Can I share designs with students / friends?

Yes — tap 📦 Export to save the design as JSON, share the file, they tap 📂 Import.

Will it get updates?

Yes. Every v1.x release is a free update. Redownload from your Etsy Purchases page anytime.

Does the app collect any data?

No. Zero accounts, zero cloud, zero analytics. Everything runs locally in your browser.

Can I use this across a whole school?

Yes — grab the Site license ($199, up to 30 teachers at one school) or the District license ($499+, multiple sites).

What if the BLE connection drops?

Tap 📡 Tap to Connect again. The app retries automatically; long-range drops usually mean distance (>10 m) or low battery.

Can I remix the built-in widgets?

Layouts, IDs, labels, sizes, min/max, colors and themes are yours to customise. New widget types require code edits to script.js.
