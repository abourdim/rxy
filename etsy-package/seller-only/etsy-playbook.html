Full description

🎮 Micro:bit Remote Builder (Rxy) — Drag. Drop. Drive. Design a Bluetooth remote control for your BBC micro:bit by dragging widgets onto a canvas, then press Play to drive the real board — no coding required. One click generates paste-ready MakeCode you drop into makecode.microbit.org to create the matching hex. WHAT YOU GET • 🧩 Drag-and-drop builder — design your own controller on a snap-to-grid canvas, no code • 🎛 12 widget types — Button · Slider · Switch · Joystick · D-Pad · XY Pad · Light · Label · Gauge · Graph · Battery · Timer • 🗂 6 starter templates — Game Pad · Robot · DJ Mixer · Race Car · Lights · Start Fresh • 🔄 Two tabs — Build (design canvas) + Play (live Bluetooth runtime) • 🧪 Auto-generated MakeCode — one click, copy, paste into MakeCode, download hex, drag onto the micro:bit • ⌨️ 20 keyboard shortcuts — Ctrl+Z/Y (undo/redo), Ctrl+C/V/D (copy/paste/duplicate), Ctrl+G (group), Ctrl+S (save template), T (cycle theme), G (grid snap), Ctrl+/- (zoom), and more • 🎨 5 themes — dark · ocean · space · candy · forest • 🌍 Trilingual UI — English · French · Arabic with full RTL • 📱 PWA — installable, offline-capable • 👶 Beginner mode + 🧙 Expert mode WHAT'S IN YOUR DOWNLOAD • index.html — the full app • makecode.ts — reference micro:bit firmware (exported from the app) • SETUP.md — 5-minute quick-start • docs/ — illustrated guide, cheat sheet, FAQ, Etsy playbook • LICENSE — personal / classroom single-user license REQUIREMENTS • BBC micro:bit V1 OR V2 — works on both (rare — most dashboards are V2-only) • Chrome or Edge on desktop (or Chrome on Android) • Bluetooth enabled • HTTPS or file:// — double-clicking the HTML works WHO IT'S FOR Teachers running STEM workshops · home-schooling families · robotics clubs · game-controller designers · makers prototyping BLE projects · anyone curious about the micro:bit. PRIVACY FIRST Nothing leaves your computer. No analytics, no logins, no cloud. All calibrations, recordings, and presets live in your browser's localStorage and on your disk. LIFETIME UPDATES Every version shipped from this listing is yours forever. Re-download the latest ZIP anytime from your Etsy Purchases page. Happy hacking! 🚀🎮🤖
